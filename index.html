Our Mission

Tech is an industry that can feel intimidating to enter. We've all experienced the frustrating conundrum of needing work experince to get work. Networking doesn't come naturally to everyone, and it can be difficult to make those initial contacts in the tech space.

We want to provide the community we wish we had when we were in university. We offer a space for students to grow with the support of their peers. We group together students from different schools & provide full-time developers as mentors; networking is organically built into our program. Students are able to add the projects they've worked on at Causmic to their resume.

Nonprofits provide their business needs and weekly check-ins. Students experience what it's like to work as developers on an Agile team.
